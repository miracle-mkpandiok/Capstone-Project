About Me

Lawyer & Web Developer.

I'm Miracle Mkpandiok, a multifaceted professional blending law and technology to craft innovative digital solutions.

A scholarship from HerTechTrail Academy presented an unexpected opportunity. Intrigued, I chose web development, despite having no prior experience. This transformative program ignited an unrelenting passion for creating responsive, user-friendly websites.

HerTechTrail Academy's training honed my technical skills and bridged the gap between law and technology. I realized the immense potential of web development in enhancing legal services, improving access to justice and driving social impact.

My legal background has refined my research, writing, resilience, problem-solving, attention to details and communication skills. These skills complement my web development expertise, enabling me to deliver exceptional solutions.

I am driven by innovation and a strong desire to continuously improve to become a better version of myself.
